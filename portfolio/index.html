Executive Case Study

Inventory Waste Risk Detection System

Production-grade operational intelligence · Zero-cost infrastructure

📌 The Problem

A multi-branch business with 36,000+ total SKUs was writing off ₦2M+ every month due to product expiry — but they had no visibility into which products were at risk, when they would expire, or how much value was actually being lost. The existing ERP lacked expiry tracking, surplus alerts, or transfer recommendations.

⚙️ The Solution

Engineered a production system deployed on Hugging Face Spaces (Streamlit frontend) with Supabase (PostgreSQL, pg_cron), Netlify serverless functions, and SMTP for automated reporting — all on free tiers. The system focuses on the 15,379 most active SKUs (representing ₦884.8M in inventory value) and delivers:

  • Daily expiry scans (90/120/180 day windows) with colour-coded risk
  • Monetary value at risk — ₦24.2M detected
  • Stock min/max monitoring with surplus & low-stock alerts
  • Cross-branch transfer recommendations
  • Automated daily 10am stakeholder email

The system replaced blind guessing with precise, auditable data — the company now knows exactly what is expiring and how much it costs.
