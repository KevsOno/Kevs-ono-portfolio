Case Study: Strategic Organic Traffic & Growth for a New E-commerce Brand

The Challenge: The online fragrance market was saturated and highly competitive. The business was a new entrant with a zero-dollar budget for launch, limited brand awareness, and no clear Product-Market Fit (PMF) for the broader, price-sensitive market.

The Lean Startup Solution (Phase 1): I architected a lean digital ecosystem using free tools. I built a functional e-commerce shop on a free WordPress.com platform and established a presence on all major social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, blogs). I implemented a custom workaround to enable customer checkout via WhatsApp, bypassing the platform's limitations without plugins.

The Strategic Rebrand (Phase 2): To achieve brand uniqueness, I conducted a deep dive using Customer Journey Mapping (CJM) and Jobs to be Done (JTBD). This analysis revealed that a growing segment was not just buying perfume; they were seeking a "personalized scent to express their identity." I used this insight to rebrand, creating content and articles that resonated with this specific customer need.

The Data-Driven Scale (Phase 3): I formally applied strategic frameworks to validate our new direction. A Blue Ocean Strategy was used to move the brand into an uncontested niche, while a TAM-SAM-SOM analysis projected a potential increase in annual revenue from ₦3M–₦6M to ₦15M–₦30M. A SWOT analysis was conducted to identify core weaknesses and build a roadmap for long-term growth.

Results

  • The strategy successfully positioned the brand in a unique market space, leading to a 46.7% organic traffic growth in two months.
  • The strategic roadmap is projected to increase the conversion rate by 10-15% and improve the brand’s Net Promoter Score (NPS) by building a loyal community.
  • Landed a high-value client, generating profit 27× the average deal size. This boosted overall profit significantly while keeping CAC lower than previous levels and improving the precision of marketing copy and targeting.
Skills & Tools used: WordPress, Social Media Platforms (FB, IG, TikTok, Pinterest), Email Marketing, CJM, JTBD, SWOT Analysis, Blue Ocean Strategy, TAM-SAM-SOM, SEO, E-commerce.
