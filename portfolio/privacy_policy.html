Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2025

KD (Kevs Digital) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable data protection laws, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA), the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation 2019 (NDPR), the UK Data Protection Act 2018, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA).

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, company details, and information you provide via forms or consultations.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, location (approx.), pages visited, referral data.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Data collected through cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you opt in to newsletters, marketing, or communications.
  • Contractual necessity: To provide services you request.
  • Legal obligations: To comply with Nigerian, UK/EU, or US laws.
  • Legitimate interests: For analytics, improving services, preventing fraud.

3. Your Rights (Nigeria NDPA/NDPR, GDPR, UK DPA)

If you are in Nigeria, the UK, or the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Data portability — request transfer of your data in a usable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at Kevs Digital .

4. Your Rights (California CCPA/CPRA)

The CCPA/CPRA applies only to residents of California, USA. If you are a California resident, you may:

  • Request disclosure of categories of data collected, used, or shared.
  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information (with exceptions).
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (Kevs Digital does not sell personal data).
  • Exercise rights without discrimination.

For clients in other US states, Kevs Digital applies GDPR-level protections, ensuring transparency, consent, and control over your personal data.

5. Global Clients & Other Jurisdictions

For clients outside Nigeria, the UK/EU, and the USA, Kevs Digital applies the same high standards of privacy protection based on GDPR principles. This means:

  • Clear transparency about what data we collect and why.
  • Requiring your consent for marketing communications.
  • Allowing you to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Applying strong security measures to protect your data.

By applying GDPR-level standards globally, we ensure that your data rights are respected no matter where you are located.

6. How We Use Information

  • Deliver and improve our services (SEO, web design, automation, digital marketing).
  • Communicate with you (consultations, updates, offers).
  • Send newsletters and promotions with consent.
  • Analyze site usage and enhance user experience.
  • Comply with legal/regulatory requirements.

7. Sharing of Information

We may share data with:

  • Service providers: email platforms, analytics, hosting, all under data processing agreements.
  • Legal/regulatory authorities: when required by law.

Kevs Digital does not sell personal data.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for service delivery, compliance, or legitimate business purposes, in line with NDPA and GDPR standards.

9. International Data Transfers

As Kevs Digital works with clients in Nigeria, the UK, EU, and US, your data may be transferred across borders. We ensure such transfers comply with NDPA, GDPR, and other relevant regulations, using safeguards such as contractual clauses and secure providers.

10. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data. While we take reasonable steps, no online system is 100% secure.

11. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If such data is collected, we will delete it immediately.
